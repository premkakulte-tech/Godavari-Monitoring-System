Satellite Data Analysis

Multi-temporal Landsat (2001, 2013) and SAR (2025) satellite imagery were used to prepare LULC maps, analyze riverbank changes, and predict future encroachment hotspots using Random Forest.

IoT Sensor System

  • Water Level Sensor: Detects rising river stages and flood risk
  • Rainfall Sensor: Measures precipitation intensity
  • Vibration Sensor: Identifies construction or ground disturbance near riverbanks

About the Project

This final year Civil Engineering project focuses on the Godavari River stretch from Someshwar to Ramkund, Nashik. The system integrates RS, GIS, SAR, Machine Learning, and IoT sensors for sustainable river management and hazard mitigation.
